How it works

From first message to fully operational — three steps

No account to create, no lengthy contract to parse. Tell us what you need, and we coordinate the rest.

Most people hear back within one business day.

Get started See pricing
01

Step one

Tell us what you need

Send a message, book a short call, or just tell us where you are in the process — already in Thailand, or still planning the move. There's no form to fill out and no account to set up first. Pick a tier that matches your situation, or mix and match individual services if you only need part of what we offer.

02

Step two

We coordinate the partners

Company registration, BOI applications, visas, and accounting are handled by licensed Thai partners — Walailak Law Firm for legal and visa work, a licensed accounting firm for your books. You don't manage three separate relationships. We stay in contact with each partner, track timelines, and translate progress into plain updates for you.

03

Step three

You're operational

Your website is live, your company is registered, your visa and work permit are in hand, and your books are running — handed off with clear access and documentation, not a pile of logins you have to figure out yourself. If you're on Tier 3, we stay on as your ongoing point of contact.

Ready to send that first message?

Tell us where you are in the process, and we'll tell you exactly what's next.
