Course Overview

Strategic Procurement & Vendor Management

Procurement functions play a critical role in organizational performance, operational continuity, and cost optimization. This in-house training program helps organizations develop more strategic procurement practices while strengthening vendor management capability.

Business Challenges Addressed

Supplier performance inconsistency
Vendor dependency risks
Procurement inefficiencies
Weak evaluation frameworks
Contract risks
Cost control pressures
Governance gaps
Limited vendor visibility

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Apply strategic sourcing principles
  • Develop vendor evaluation frameworks
  • Strengthen supplier relationship management
  • Improve vendor performance monitoring
  • Manage procurement risks
  • Improve negotiation capability
  • Strengthen contract alignment
