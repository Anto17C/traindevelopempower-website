Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our in-house corporate training courses.

Can these programs be customized for our organization's in-house training courses?
Yes. All training programs can be fully customized to align with your industry, operational environment, workforce capability requirements, and specific business objectives. We work with you to tailor content, case studies, and delivery format to your context.
Do you deliver these in-house training courses onsite or virtually?
We offer flexible delivery options including onsite training at your location, live virtual instructor-led sessions, and hybrid learning solutions that combine both. The format is selected based on your workforce requirements and organizational preferences.
Which industries do you serve?
Our in-house training solutions support a wide range of industries including oil and gas, manufacturing, logistics and transport, utilities and energy, government and public sector, construction and engineering, banking and financial services, healthcare, ports and maritime, retail, corporate services, and education.
How long are your in-house training programs?
Program duration is flexible and tailored to your requirements. Most in-house training programs run between 1 and 5 days depending on the topic, depth, and your organizational context. We can adapt program length to fit your schedule and learning objectives.
How do we request an in-house training proposal?
You can contact us through our Request Proposal form, by email at iht@traindevelopempower.com, by phone at +66 90 324 7862, or via WhatsApp. Our team will discuss your requirements and develop a customized training proposal for your organization.
