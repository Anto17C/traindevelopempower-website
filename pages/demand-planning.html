Course Overview

Demand Planning & Supply Forecasting

Demand uncertainty, market volatility, and supply chain complexity challenge organizational planning and forecasting capability. This in-house training program helps organizations strengthen demand planning and supply forecasting capability while improving planning visibility and forecasting reliability.

Business Challenges Addressed

Forecast inaccuracies
Demand volatility
Poor planning coordination
Inventory imbalances
Weak supply-demand alignment
Planning visibility gaps
Reactive decision-making
Stock shortages and overstocking

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Apply demand planning principles
  • Select appropriate forecasting methods
  • Improve supply-demand alignment
  • Strengthen inventory planning capability
  • Manage demand variability
  • Develop effective S&OP practices
