Course Overview

Organizations that invest in coaching and mentoring create stronger leaders, improve employee engagement, accelerate capability development, and achieve higher levels of organizational performance. Effective coaching and mentoring enable managers and leaders to develop people, unlock potential, improve accountability, and build future leadership capability.

This in-house training program is designed to help organizations strengthen coaching and mentoring skills through practical coaching frameworks, mentoring techniques, communication strategies, feedback methodologies, and performance development approaches. Participants will learn how to support employee growth, improve workplace performance, and build stronger professional relationships that contribute to long-term organizational success.

The Coaching & Mentoring for Performance program combines proven coaching and mentoring principles with practical tools and methodologies that help leaders develop talent, improve engagement, and strengthen organizational capability.

Business Challenges Addressed
  • Limited employee development opportunities
  • Weak coaching and feedback practices
  • Low employee engagement and motivation
  • Inconsistent performance management approaches
  • Leadership succession challenges
  • Skills and capability gaps
  • Poor knowledge transfer between employees
  • Difficulty retaining high-potential talent
  • Limited career development support
  • Weak learning and development cultures
Key Learning Outcomes
  • Understand coaching and mentoring principles and methodologies
  • Differentiate between coaching, mentoring, and managing
  • Apply effective coaching conversations and techniques
  • Improve employee performance through structured coaching
  • Build productive mentoring relationships
  • Strengthen communication and active listening skills
  • Deliver constructive feedback confidently
  • Support employee growth and career development
  • Improve engagement, accountability, and motivation
  • Create a culture of continuous learning and development
