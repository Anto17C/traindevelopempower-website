Course Overview

Global supply chains continue to face growing disruption exposure driven by geopolitical instability, economic uncertainty, supplier dependency, transportation bottlenecks, cyber risks, regulatory changes, and operational volatility. Organizations across infrastructure-heavy and industrial sectors are under increasing pressure to improve visibility, continuity, agility, and resilience across their supply chain operations.

This in-house training program is designed to help organizations strengthen supply chain resilience strategies, improve risk identification capabilities, and enhance operational continuity planning in complex business environments. Participants will explore practical approaches to managing supply chain vulnerabilities, assessing disruption exposure, improving supplier risk visibility, and building more resilient operational frameworks.

The Supply Chain Resilience program combines strategic supply chain resilience concepts with operationally focused risk management practices relevant to modern organizations operating across dynamic, regional, and global markets.

Business Challenges Addressed
  • Supply chain disruption exposure
  • Supplier dependency and concentration risks
  • Inventory volatility and lead-time uncertainty
  • Operational continuity planning gaps
  • Lack of supply chain visibility
  • Global sourcing vulnerabilities
  • Transportation and logistics instability
  • Risk identification and mitigation gaps
  • Crisis response coordination challenges
  • Cost versus resilience balancing pressures
Key Learning Outcomes
  • Identify key supply chain risks and vulnerabilities
  • Evaluate organizational supply chain resilience maturity
  • Improve supplier risk assessment and monitoring practices
  • Develop operational continuity and response strategies
  • Strengthen supply chain visibility and risk reporting processes
  • Analyse disruption operational performance impact
  • Improve crisis response coordination across supply chain functions
  • Implement practical resilience frameworks and mitigation approaches
  • Support strategic decision-making under uncertain operating conditions
  • Align resilience planning with organizational operational priorities
