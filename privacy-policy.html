2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal and business contact information when you submit a proposal request, email us, call us, message us through WhatsApp, or otherwise communicate with us. This may include your name, job title, organization, country, email address, phone number, training interests, message content, and related business requirements.

When you use our website, basic technical information may also be processed by our website hosting, security, and form service providers, such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, form submission time, and anti-spam checks.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to respond to enquiries, prepare proposals, communicate about training requirements, arrange meetings or calls, deliver agreed services, maintain business records, improve our website and services, prevent spam or abuse, and comply with applicable legal or administrative obligations.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use proposal form details for unrelated marketing without a suitable basis or your consent where required.

4. Sharing and Service Providers

We may share information with trusted service providers that help us operate the website, receive form submissions, manage email, host files, protect against spam, or administer business systems. These providers process information only as needed to provide their services.

Our current website and communication setup may involve providers such as Cloudflare for hosting, DNS, security, and website delivery; Web3Forms for proposal form handling; and Zoho for business email and workplace services.

We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect our rights, clients, users, or business operations.

5. Retention and Security

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, including responding to enquiries, preparing proposals, managing client relationships, maintaining records, resolving disputes, or meeting legal and accounting requirements.

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. However, no website, email system, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You may contact us to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of the personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable law and legitimate business or legal retention needs.

You may also ask us not to use your contact details for future non-essential communications. To make a request, contact us at iht@traindevelopempower.com.

7. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use essential technologies required for security, hosting, form submission, or normal website operation. If we add analytics, advertising pixels, or similar tracking tools in the future, we may update this policy and, where required, provide additional notice or choices.

8. International Processing

Because our website, email, form, and cloud service providers may operate in multiple countries, personal information may be processed or stored outside Thailand. We take reasonable steps to use reputable providers and protect information in line with this policy and applicable requirements.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, systems, legal requirements, or business practices. The effective date at the top of this page shows when the policy was last updated.
